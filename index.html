About Us

About Us

Aqsa Group Foundation - Helping Communities, Saving Lives

At Aqsa Group Foundation, our mission is to provide essential services to those in need, including blood donation drives, food distribution at railway stations and hospitals, and providing critical fluid aids to patients. We aim to make a real impact in the community by reaching out to the most vulnerable.

  • Blood Donation Camps

  • Food Distribution at Railway Stations

  • Fluid Aid for Patients

Join Our Growing Network of 324+ Partners

At Aqsa Group Foundation, we are committed to making a difference through our humanitarian services. From blood donation camps to medical aid, we strive to help those in need and save lives. Join us in our mission to bring hope and relief to communities around the world.

What We Do

We Work Differently to Make a Difference

  • Blood Donation Camps

    We organize blood donation camps to help save lives and support local hospitals in need of blood supplies.

  • Community Support

    We provide food distribution services in railway stations and hospitals to ensure no one goes hungry.

  • Medical Aid

    We assist underprivileged patients by providing medical aid and essential supplies to those in need.

  • Awareness Campaigns

    We run awareness campaigns on health, hygiene, and nutrition to educate communities and empower them.
